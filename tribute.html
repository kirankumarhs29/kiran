Bhagath singh

An Indian Fighter

Bhagat Singh was an Indian socialist revolutionary whose two acts of dramatic violence against the British in India and execution at age 23 made him a folk hero of the Indian independence movement

Here's the timeline of Bhagath Singh

  • Born in 28th September
  • 1919 when he was 12 years old, Singh visited the site of the Jallianwala Bagh massacre hours after thousands of unarmed people gathered at a public meeting had been killed
  • 1926he founded the Indian socialist youth organisation Naujawan Bharat Sabha
  • 1931 he was hanged at age of 23
